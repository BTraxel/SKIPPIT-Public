Account Deletion Instructions

To request the deletion of your SKIPPIT account, please send us an email at: skippitfeedback@gmail.com with the email address associated with your account. We will verify your identity before proceeding to ensure the security of your account. Upon confirmation, we will process your request as quickly as possible.

Your profile and personal data will be deleted instantly upon request. However, unless specified otherwise, comments you left on the app will be kept for a maximum duration of 2 years. This practice is in compliance with our data retention policy, designed to comply with all major privacy laws, including GDPR, CCPA, and others. We are committed to maintaining the highest standards of privacy and data protection.

For more details, please refer to our Privacy Policy.
